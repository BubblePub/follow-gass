# 事件概括 Prompt (summarize-event)

For each event cluster, write a summary in **Chinese**. Structure, in this order:

## 1. 事件概括 (event summary)
2–4 sentences in Chinese. What happened, who did/said what, why it matters. Base this
ONLY on the titles and snippets in the feed — you have headlines + short snippets, not
full articles. Do NOT invent details, quotes, or numbers that aren't in the snippets.
If the snippets are thin, keep the summary short rather than padding.

## 2. 来源 (source link list)
List every distinct source in the cluster, one per line, as:

  - [来源媒体] 法语原标题 — URL

Keep the **original French (or English) title** verbatim next to the outlet name, then
the link. This is the reader's gateway to the original reporting. Order tier-1 outlets
first. Every line MUST have a real URL from the feed — no URL, no line.

## Rules
- Never fabricate. Headlines/snippets only.
- Don't reproduce long passages — a headline title is fine, but never paste article
  paragraphs.
- If the same outlet appears twice with near-identical pieces, keep one.
- Keep the person's name and role correct: Attal = 复兴党总书记/2027 总统初选参选人;
  Séjourné = 欧盟委员会执行副主席(繁荣与产业战略).
