# 翻译 Prompt (translate)

When rendering French/English source material into Chinese:

- Keep **proper nouns in their original form** the first time, with a Chinese gloss if
  helpful: 复兴党 (Renaissance)、国民议会 (Assemblée nationale)、欧盟委员会 (Commission
  européenne). People's names stay in Latin script: Gabriel Attal、Stéphane Séjourné.
- **Media titles are NOT translated** — they appear in the source list in their original
  French/English, verbatim. Only the 概括 (summary) is in Chinese.
- Translate institutional/policy terms accurately, not literally:
  - "Vice-président exécutif" → 执行副主席
  - "stratégie industrielle" → 产业战略
  - "Made in Europe" → 保留英文,可加注「欧洲制造」
  - "groupe Renaissance à l'Assemblée" → 国民议会复兴党团
- Keep a neutral, factual register — this is a news digest, not commentary. Do not
  inject opinion or loaded adjectives that aren't in the source.
- Numbers, dates, and named laws: keep precise; if unsure from the snippet, omit rather
  than guess.
